Orchid

The flower symbolism associated with the orchid is love, beauty, refinement, many children, thoughtfulness and mature charm.

Cherry blossom

Once the cherry blossom trees have flowered, their delicate beauty usually only lasts a week, with the petals constantly falling to the ground and carpeting it in a swathe of pink. It's also part of why the blossoms can represent fragility and fleeting beauty in Japanese culture.
